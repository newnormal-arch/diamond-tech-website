About Us

Our business model is to partner with global ICT brands since 2018. Founded by Fortune Makoni, we are a proudly globally youth owned company.Our services include cloud services, data backup, business continuity and disaster recovery, ICT strategy and planning, network operations, cybersecurity and compliance or Office 365 design.

Our Products & Services

Diamond Tech is an ICT company that sells and supports a wide range of hardware, software, consumables, and electrical products. We also offer IT consulting, IT support, and IT training services. We are committed to providing our customers with the best possible products and services at competitive prices.

Diamond Tech is a great option for businesses of all sizes that are looking for high-quality IT products and services.

PC's & Laptops

We are suppliers of the biggest PC and Laptop brands such as Apple, HP, Mecer etc. We also supply PC and Laptop spare parts such as screens and accessories.

Cellphone's & Tablets

We are a leading retailer of cellphones and tablets of the world's leading brands such as Apple, Huawei and many more. We sell brand new and second hand phones.

Printers & Cartridges

We are suppliers of commercial and domestic printers such as HP, Canon and Fijitsu. Our cartridges also come with a 180 day money back guarantee.
